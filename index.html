Keynote Speaker

Dr. Racheal Brooks

Director of Quality Assurance Implementation Solutions at Quality Matters

Keynote Time: 12:30 PM - 1:15 PM

Dr. Racheal Brooks is a seasoned higher education administrator, educator, and consultant with over 16 years of experience dedicated to advancing teaching and learning. She is the Director of Quality Assurance Implementation Solutions at Quality Matters, where she leads consulting efforts and supports institutions in strengthening online quality assurance. As a faculty member, she teaches online and blended Spanish and graduate-level research methods courses. Previously, Dr. Brooks served as Director of the Office of e-Learning at North Carolina Central University (NCCU) and Co-Chair of the UNC System Quality Matters Council.

🚐 Free Shuttle Service

The shuttle has been reserved for the entire day of the QM Summit and will run round-trip every 20 minutes between 9:00 am and 6:00 pm from the Latham Parking Deck to the Student Center.

Need assistance? Contact Transportation Dispatch Office at (919) 530-5139 for more information or to have a shuttle dispatched to you.

Parking Options at Latham Deck:

👩‍🏫 Faculty & Staff with Decals

Park in designated non-hourly spaces on levels 2-5

👥 Visitors

Attendees who are driving will be able to pull a ticket upon entry to the Latham parking deck. The cost is $1.50 per hour, and individuals can pay upon exiting the parking deck.

The deck accepts payments through ParkMobile.

💡 Our Recommendation

For the most convenient experience, we recommend using the Latham Parking Deck with the free shuttle service. This ensures guaranteed parking and easy transportation to the Student Center.
